Hi, we're Greenbox.

Greenbox was born many years ago in a small green gouse in a faraway tropical climatte, during a gap year after studies. Beign a predominantly remote set up in the early years, Greenbox eventually set down some preoper roots in Cape Town, South Africa in 2009.

Now, 8 years later Greenbox has steadily grown into a well established web design agency in Cape Town.

Being a smaller team, we can really offer a hands on personalized experience to elevate your brand, and provide you with a fresh web presence which is crucial in today's ever changing technology climate.

"We'r All about innovation. In this fast-paced industry where technology reigns and evolves with every passing second, it's imperative that your brand remains relevant, and immune to invisibility"

Our team has experience and expertise to handle any web related project, from boutique graphic design, custom Wordpress development to full scale Drupal application development.
